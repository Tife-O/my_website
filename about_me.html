About Me

Hello! My name is Oluwatifemiju (Tife) Odepe. I use he/him/his pronouns. I am currently a rising college freshman at Rutgers-New Brunswick majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering. I love trying new things, and recently, I have developed a strong passion for programming, and have become proficient in JavaScript and Python.



In my free time, I love to play basketball, read, watch movies, watch baseball, play instruments, listen to music, and play video games. I am a huge fan of anything Marvel, Star Wars, or Harry Potter related, and will dive into the various theories of each cinematic universe if someone doesn't stop me. I speak English, French, and Yoruba, currently working on ASL.



I was born in the United Kingdom, then moved to Nigeria two months later. I lived in Lagos for six years before moving again to Canada, where I completed the majority of my primary and secondary school. Ten years later, I finished high school in New Jersey, and now, I enthusiastically prepare for the next step in my academic journey: college!
